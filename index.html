About the Founder

Diamond is derived from the Greek word “adamas”, which means invincible.
According to Merriam-Webster, the meaning of adamant is "an unbreakable or an
extremely hard substance."

Diamonds IQ Network was founded by Christopher Park, a member of Mensa.

The network was founded to reward test takers for demonstrating persistence
on and unwavering commitment to taking IQ tests.

Admission:

Diamonds (IQ cut off: 162 s.d. 15):

  1. Official: IQ ≥ 162 (s.d. 15) on at least 6 acceptable IQ tests
    1. Eminent: IQ ≥ 169 (s.d. 15) on at least 2 acceptable IQ tests

Double Diamonds (IQ cut off: 174 s.d. 15):

  1. Official: IQ ≥ 174 (s.d. 15) on at least 6 acceptable IQ tests
